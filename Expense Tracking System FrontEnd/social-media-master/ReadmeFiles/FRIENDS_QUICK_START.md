## ğŸ”„ Common Workflows

### Workflow 1: Clean Up Friend Requests

**Scenario**: You sent requests to many people, but some never responded.

**Steps**:

1. Go to **Requests** tab
2. Switch to **Outgoing** sub-tab
3. Review all pending requests
4. Click **Cancel** on outdated requests
5. Your outgoing count updates automatically

**Benefit**: Keeps your requests list clean and organized

---

### Workflow 2: Remove Unwanted Connections

**Scenario**: Someone added you but you don't want to share with them anymore.

**Steps**:

1. Go to **My Friends** tab
2. Find the person
3. Click **â‹®** on their card
4. Choose action:
   - **Unfriend**: Just remove connection
   - **Block**: Prevent future requests too
5. Confirm action

**When to Unfriend vs Block**:

- **Unfriend**: You don't want to share anymore, but they're not bothering you
- **Block**: They're sending unwanted requests or you want to completely cut off interaction

---

### Workflow 3: Reconnect with Blocked User

**Scenario**: You blocked someone by mistake or want to give them another chance.

**Steps**:

1. Go to **Blocked** tab
2. Find the user
3. Click **Unblock** button
4. They can now send friend requests again
5. You can also send them a request from Suggestions

**Note**: Unblocking doesn't restore the friendship. You need to add them again.

---

### Workflow 4: Monitor Your Friendship Activity

**Scenario**: You want to see your overall friendship status.

**Steps**:

1. Look at the **Stats Card** at the top
2. Click on any stat card for detailed view
3. Review:
   - Total friends
   - Pending incoming requests (need your response)
   - Pending outgoing requests (waiting for their response)
   - Blocked users
   - Expense sharing summary

**Use Case**: Regular checkups to manage your network

## âš ï¸ Important Notes

### Things to Know

1. **Blocking Removes Friendship**

   - If you block someone you're friends with, the friendship is removed first
   - You'll need to unblock AND send a new request to reconnect

2. **Unfriend is Permanent**

   - Unfriending removes all shared expense access
   - You'll need to re-add them and set up sharing again

3. **Cancel vs Reject**

   - **Cancel**: Remove YOUR outgoing request (you sent it)
   - **Reject**: Deny THEIR incoming request (they sent it)

4. **Statistics Auto-Update**

   - Stats refresh automatically when you take actions
   - No need to manually refresh

5. **Empty States**
   - Each tab shows helpful messages when empty
   - Blocked tab shows encouragement message when no one is blocked

### âœ… DO

- Check stats card regularly to stay on top of pending requests
